INTO THE DREAMLANDS

Into the dreamlands is a platformer and puzle couch coop split screen game inspired by It takes two games. This game portrays the story of a girl who is going through the phase where she starts to leave her childhood imagination to become a teenager. Each of the players controls a character, the girl and the other being her imaginary friend. The main mechanic of this game is that the two characters see the environment in different ways. This mechanic is supported by the child's imagination, which diminishes throughout the game.
Time spent on this project: 4 months until now
Engine: Unreal Engine 5

DODGE GAME


Dodge game is a game inspired by games like Super Hexagone and Shapes and Beats. In this game the player can choose between two game modes. Levels, 8 levels that increase the difficulty where the player has to endure 30 seconds without being hit by any object. Survive, endless mode where the player will add points the longer they stay in the game. This game has as mechanics a dash and reflect, which allows the player to reflect a ball that destroys any object it touches.
Time spent on this project: 3 months
Engine: Unity

BABEL

Babel is a first person arena type game where you can throw a dagger and teleport to it at any time. In this game the objective is to progress in the tower of babel passing through the different floors that have different enemies. In this game the player loses life overtime having to kill enemies to recover life. In addition, it may be necessary to activate pressure plates to complete the level. This game has a boss.
Time spent on this project: 3 months
Engine: Unity
Website with download

DISTURBING SITE

Disturbing site is a game where our only objective is to annoy the workers on a construction site, stealing materials so that the construction site cannot be completed. But to complicate matters, there are workers who keep watch over the area, having to hide from them while we steal the materials, increasing the time that the work will need to be completed. In addition, there is the mechanic of placing a trap on the ground so that workers remain stationary for a few seconds.
Time spent on this project: 15 hours
Engine: Unreal Engine 5
